Food Portal

Food Portal is a digital platform that brings culinary delights to your fingertips. Whether you’re a food lover eager to explore a variety of dishes or an admin managing the portal, it makes the experience seamless and enjoyable.

Admin Access: Secure login allows admins to manage orders, update the menu, and control the portal efficiently.

Ordering: Users can browse a diverse menu, place orders with a few clicks, and enjoy a hassle-free dining experience.

Mission: To create a connected food community, promoting diverse cuisines and making food discovery easy for everyone.

Description:

I built Food Portal as a Python CLI application with a SQL database backend. This project allows users to browse menus, place orders, and enjoy a seamless ordering experience. Admins can manage orders, update menus, and control the portal efficiently. The project aims to create a connected food community and make discovering new cuisines simple and enjoyable.

Skills: Python, MySQL, Backend Development, Database Design, Problem Solving.
